Z

  1. Zaijian = See you around, bye 
  2. Zai = to be in,on, or at 
  3. zenme = how
  4. zenmeyang = how
  5. Zhe = this
  6. Zhongwu = Afternoon
  7. zhu = live or alive
  8. zhou zi = desk
  9. zi = character or word
  10. zoutian = yesterday
  11. zou = sit
  12. zou = make

     

     

     

    A

    Ai - love

    Mandarin: Wo ai ji kou ren

    English: I love my family.



     

    B

    Ba- eight

    Mandarin:{Ni ji sui?, Wo ba sui}

    English:(How old are you? I am eight.)

     

    Baba- daddy

    Mandarin:Wo de baba si shi sui

     English:(My dad is forty years old)

     

    Beizi- cup or glass

    Mandarin: Beizi zai yi zi xian mian

     English:(The cup is under the chair)

     

    Ben- books

    Mandarin:Yi ben shu-

    English: (A book)

     

    Bu keqi- Welcome or it is okay

    Mandarin:{DU bu qi.i}Bu ke qi

    English:(One person is saying sorry and the other person is saying that it is okay)

     

    Bu = not/no

    Mandarin:(Wo bu kaijian)

    English: I am not very well





     

    C

    Cai-dish cuisine

    Mandarin: Wo ai zhongguo cai 

    English: I love chinese food

     

    Cha- tea

    Mandarin: Wo xiang he cha

     English: I would like some tea

     

    Chi - to eat

    Mandarin: Wo xiang chi mifan

    English: I’d like some rice

     

    Chuzuche-taxi

    Mandarin: Wo men shi zuo chuzuche lai de

    English: We came by taxi

    Y

    Yi = one

    Mandarin: Wo de nu er yi sui

    English: My daughter is 1 year old

     

    Yifu-clothes

    Mandarin: Lan se de yifu du shao qian

    English: How much do these Blue clothes cost?

     

    Yi sheng- doctor

    Mandarin: Yi sheng!Yi sheng zai nar?

    English: Doctor! Where is the doctor?

     

    Yiyuan- hospital

    Mandarin: Wo de mama yiyuan gonzou.

    English: My mom works at hospital

     

    Yi zi-Chair

    Mandarin : Yi zi zai na er

    English:WHere is the chair

     

    Yi dianr = a few,little

    Mandarin: Wo de yi fu yi dian er

    English: I have very minimal clothes

     

    You = to have/there be

    Mandarin:Senlin limian you lang.

    English:There are wolves in this forest

     

    Yue = month

    Mandarin: Wo de sheng ri liu yue er shi wu hao

    English: My birthday is in June 25

    X

    Xue xiao-school

    Mandarin: Wo xihuan xuexiao\

    English: I am fond of school

     

    Xue xi = study

    Mandarin: Wo ai xuexi

    English: I like to study

     

    Xuesheng- student

    Mandarin: Wo de Hanyu xuesheng

    English: I am a chinese student 

     

    Xingxi = week

    Mandarin:Mingtian xing xi yi

    English: Tommorow is Monday

     

    Xie Xie = Thank you

    Mandarin: ren shi de hen gao xing xie xie

    English: Very nice ot meet you, thank you




    Xie-write

    Mandarin: Wo hui hanzi xie

    English: I can write chinese characters

     

    Xie-few/little

    Mandarin: Neng gei wo xie bing ma?

    English: Can you get me some ice?

     

     

    Xiao jie = Miss or young lady

    Mandarin: ta de nuer xiaojie

    English: her daughter is a young lady

     

    Xiao = small

    Mandarin:Ta de er zi hen xiao

    English: His son is very little

     

    Xiang  = Want

    Mandarin:Wo de xiang tongguo Hsk

    English: I want to pass HSK

     

    Xian zai = now

    Mandarin: Xianzai you si ge dianshi pindao.

    English: There are currently 4 television channels

     

    Xian sheng = Mr. or sir

    Mandarin: Xiansheng Dawei zai nar

    English: Where is Mr. David

     

    Xia yu = rainy

    Mandarin: Mingtian tianqi hen xiayu

    English: Tomorrow the weather will be very rainy

     

    Xia wu = afternoon

    Mandarin: Mingtian xiawu tian qi bu leng bu re

    English: Tommorrow afternoon it will be not to hot and not too cold

     

    Xia - Under

    Mandarin:Wo de bei zi xiamian zho zi

    English: The cup is under the table

     

    Xi huan = like
