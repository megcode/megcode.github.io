HSK R

Renshi = to meet
Mandarin: Wo de hengaoxing renshi ni
English: Very happy to see you

Ren = human
Mandarin: Ni shi na guo ren
English: Where are you from

Re = hot
Mandarin: Mingtian tianqi bu leng bu re
English: Tommorow it will be not too hot and not too cold

HSK Q

Qu = to go
Mandarin: Ni qu haishi bu qu
English: Are you going or not?

Qing  = please(polite way of asking)
Mandarin: Qing ba ni de shou gei wu
ENglish: Give me one of your hands

Qian = money
Mandarin: Qing gei wo qian
English: Give me some money

Qianmian = front
Mandarin: Wo de mao yi zi de qianmia
English: My cat is in front of the chair

qi = seven
Mandarin: wo qi sui
English: I am seven years

pengyou = friends
Mandarin: Wo de pengyoujiu sui
